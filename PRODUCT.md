# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

adaptive

## Users

Small business owners and professionals sharing profiles (vCards) or links. They need a premium way to present themselves physically and digitally, requiring highly customized landing pages and easily scanable, beautiful QR codes.

## Product Purpose

Thateasy_qr is a premium, unified SaaS platform designed to generate, customize, manage, and track dynamic and static QR codes. It enables users to bridge their offline presence with an elegant online footprint (vCards, link trees, and files) with full scan analytics.

## Positioning

Generating beautiful, bespoke QR codes that feel like an extension of a high-end personal brand.

## Brand Personality

Elegant and editorial (clean serif typography, warm tones, quiet confidence).

## Anti-references

Cheap, ad-heavy, or spammy public QR generators.

## Design Principles

- **Quiet Confidence**: Avoid loud, aggressive SaaS visuals, distracting animations, and busy widgets. Rely on clean layout grids, ample whitespace, and elegant typography.
- **Bespoke Craftsmanship**: Every generated QR code, public vCard, link tree, and file page must look premium and feel like a high-end publication, not a generic template.
- **Intentional Simplicity**: Ensure the dashboard, creation wizard, and styling customizer feel effortless, clear, and highly polished, hiding complexity while maximizing control.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Targeting standard WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines. The UI must maintain strong color contrast (especially on cream/neutral backgrounds) and highly readable typography for both dashboard users and public mobile visitors.
